### Glossary

- **TGE (Token Generation Event)**: The date when tokens are first minted and distributed.
- **FDV (Fully Diluted Valuation)**: Total supply Ã— current price (max possible market cap).
- **Circulating Supply**: Tokens currently tradable (excludes locked/vesting tokens).
- **Liquidity Pool**: Smart contract holding token pairs for decentralized trading (e.g., FLO/USDC).
- **Slippage**: Price impact when executing a trade (higher for large orders).
- **APY (Annual Percentage Yield)**: Annualized return from staking (includes compounding).
- **Vesting**: Gradual release of tokens over time (prevents sudden dumps).
- **Cliff**: Period before first token unlock (e.g., 12-month cliff = no tokens for 1 year).
- **Market Maker**: Entity providing liquidity to maintain tight bid-ask spreads.
