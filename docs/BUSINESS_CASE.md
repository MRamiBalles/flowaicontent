### Market Risks

**1. OpenAI/Google Launch Competing Product**
- **Probability**: Medium (40%)
- **Impact**: High
- **Mitigation**: Network effects + token lock-in make switching costly. Focus on community, not just tech.

**2. Regulation of AI-Generated Content**
- **Probability**: Medium (50%)
- **Impact**: Medium
- **Mitigation**: Watermarking, content moderation, DMCA compliance. Built-in from Day 1.

**3. Crypto Winter / Token Volatility**
- **Probability**: High (60%)
- **Impact**: Low-Medium
- **Mitigation**: Token economy is <10% of revenue. Can operate without it. Stablecoin option for risk-averse users.

### Execution Risks

**4. Scaling Infrastructure Costs**
- **Probability**: Medium (50%)
- **Impact**: Medium
- **Mitigation**: Multi-cloud strategy (AWS + RunPod). Reserved instances. Optimize models for efficiency.

**5. Key Team Member Departure**
- **Probability**: Low (20%)
- **Impact**: High
- **Mitigation**: Equity vesting, strong culture, succession planning.

**6. Security Breach / Data Leak**
- **Probability**: Low (10%)
- **Impact**: Critical
- **Mitigation**: SOC 2 audit (Year 2), bug bounty program, third-party pen testing.

### Competitive Risks

**7. Adobe Acquires Runway or Pika**
- **Probability**: Medium (40%)
