## ðŸš§ Risks & Mitigation

### High-Impact Risks

#### Risk 1: OpenAI/Google Launch Competing Product
**Probability**: Medium (40%)  
**Impact**: Critical  
**Mitigation**:
- Focus on community + token economy (not just tech)
- Build defensible moats (network effects, creator lock-in)
- Differentiate on creator monetization, not just generation

#### Risk 2: Regulation of AI Content or Tokens
**Probability**: Medium (50%)  
**Impact**: High  
**Mitigation**:
- Watermark all AI content (transparency)
- Legal compliance upfront (GDPR, token legal opinions)
- Diversify revenue (not dependent on tokens)

#### Risk 3: Smart Contract Exploit
**Probability**: Low (10%)  
**Impact**: Critical  
**Mitigation**:
- Multi-audits (CertiK + OpenZeppelin)
- Bug bounty program ($100k pool)
- Insurance policy ($10M coverage)

#### Risk 4: Key Team Member Departure
**Probability**: Low (20%)  
**Impact**: High  
**Mitigation**:
- Equity vesting (4-year with 1-year cliff)
- Succession planning (document tribal knowledge)
- Strong culture + competitive compensation
