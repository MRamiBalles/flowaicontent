## 1. Value Proposition (USP)
**"Unleash Your Creative Flow with AI"**
FlowAI is the first decentralized, AI-powered content creation studio that empowers creators to **Create, Monetize, and Own** their digital assets. Unlike traditional tools, we combine professional-grade Video Generation with Web3 ownership.

### Key Differentiators:
-   **Own Your AI Models**: Train custom Style Packs and monetize them.
-   **Fractional Ownership**: Mint videos as NFTs and split ownership/revenue.
-   **Gamified Creation**: Earn XP and rewards for daily creation streaks.

## 2. Target Audience
| Segment | Pain Point | FlowAI Solution |

| **Content Creators** | Burnout, slow editing, platform risk | AI automation, owned assets (NFTs) |
| **Digital Artists** | Difficulty monetizing style | Style Pack Marketplace |
| **Streamers** | Need for engagement | Co-Streaming AI Raids |
| **Brands/Agencies** | High production costs | Low-cost, high-quality AI video generation |

## 3. Product-Led Growth (PLG) Tactics
-   **Freemium Model**: Free tier allows limited daily generation to get users "hooked".
-   **Watermark Virality**: Free videos include "Made with FlowAI" watermark (link to signup).
-   **Referral Program 2.0**: Users earn "Credits" for inviting friends who subscribe.
-   **Community Challenges**: Weekly themes (e.g., "Cyberpunk Week") with NFT prizes.
