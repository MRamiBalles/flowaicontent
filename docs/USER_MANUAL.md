## ðŸŽ¬ Video Studio

### Creating Your First Video

**Step 1: Access Video Studio**
1. Click **"Video Studio"** in the sidebar
2. You'll see:
   - Prompt editor (left)
   - Preview window (right)
   - Generation queue (bottom)

**Step 2: Write a Prompt**

A prompt is a text description of your desired video.

**Good Prompts** (specific & descriptive):
```
âœ… "A cyberpunk city at night with neon lights, rain, and flying cars"
âœ… "A golden retriever puppy playing in a sunlit flower field"
âœ… "An astronaut floating in space with Earth in the background"
```

**Bad Prompts** (too vague):
```
âŒ "A city"
âŒ "Something cool"
```

**Pro Tips**:
- Mention colors, lighting, mood
- Use adjectives (epic, serene, dramatic)
- Include camera angles (close-up, wide shot)
- Keep under 200 characters

**Step 3: Select a Style**

Styles change the visual aesthetic of your video.

**Available Styles**:
- **Cinematic**: Movie-like, dramatic
- **Anime**: Japanese animation
- **Realistic**: Photorealistic
- **Retro VHS**: 1980s camcorder
- **Low Poly**: 3D geometric art
- **Oil Painting**: Classical art

**Example**: Same prompt, different styles = completely different videos!

**Step 4: Generate**

1. Click **"Generate Video"** (purple button)
2. Wait 60-90 seconds (progress bar shows status)
3. Video appears in preview window

**Behind the Scenes**:
- AI model (Stable Video Diffusion) processes prompt
- Generates 48 frames (2 seconds @ 24fps)
- Stitches frames into MP4
- Uploads to your account

**Step 5: Download or Share**

- **Download**: Save as MP4 (720p or 1080p)
- **Share**: Post to Clip Feed (public)
- **Regenerate**: Try a different style
- **Delete**: Remove from library

## ðŸ”§ Troubleshooting

### Common Issues & Solutions

#### Issue 1: Video Generation Stuck on "Processing"

**Symptoms**: Progress bar frozen, no video after 10+ minutes

**Possible Causes**:
- High server load
- Complex prompt
- System error

**Solutions**:
1. **Wait 5 more minutes** (sometimes just slow)
2. **Cancel & retry** with simpler prompt
3. **Check status page**: https://status.flowai.com
4. **Try different style** (some styles faster)
5. **Contact support** if stuck >15 minutes

---

#### Issue 2: Can't Log In

**Symptoms**: "Invalid credentials" error

**Possible Causes**:
- Wrong email/password
- Email not verified
- Account suspended

**Solutions**:
1. **Reset password**: Click "Forgot Password"
2. **Check email verification**: Resend verification email
3. **Try magic link**: Passwordless login
4. **Contact support**: If account suspended

---

#### Issue 3: Token Purchase Failed

**Symptoms**: Payment declined, no tokens received

**Possible Causes**:
- Credit card declined
- Insufficient funds
- Fraud protection triggered
- KYC required (large purchases)

**Solutions**:
1. **Check card balance**: Ensure sufficient funds
2. **Try different card**: Some banks block crypto-related purchases
3. **Contact bank**: May need to approve transaction
4. **Use PayPal** (coming Q2 2025)
5. **KYC verification**: For purchases >$1,000 (email sent)

---

#### Issue 4: Low Video Quality

**Symptoms**: Blurry, pixelated, low resolution

**Possible Causes**:
- Free tier (720p limit)
- Upscaled from lower resolution
- Vague prompt

**Solutions**:
1. **Upgrade to PRO**: 1080p native resolution
2. **Be more specific**: Detailed prompts = sharper output
3. **Try different style**: Some styles sharper than others
4. **External upscaling**: Use Topaz Video Enhance AI

---

#### Issue 5: My Video Was Removed

**Symptoms**: Video deleted, warning email received

**Possible Causes**:
- Violated Community Guidelines
- Copyright infringement
- NSFW content
- Spam/misleading

**What to Do**:
1. **Read email**: We explain reason for removal
2. **Appeal**: If you think it's a mistake, email support@flowai.com
3. **Review Guidelines**: https://flowai.com/guidelines
4. **Don't repeat violation**: Repeated violations = account suspension
